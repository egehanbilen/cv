ObGynAssist™ Developer

obgynassist.com

A clinical decision-support companion for obstetrics & gynecology, built on a deterministic, guideline-traceable engine — on-device, reproducible, and fully sourced. Spans ten integrated modules across fetal growth assessment (FGR / SGA / AGA / LGA staging, Doppler and twin pathways), pregnancy risk calculators (dating, ectopic workup, aspirin/VTE prophylaxis, thyroid, hyperemesis, CMV), and gynecologic workups — designed for clinicians at the point of care.
